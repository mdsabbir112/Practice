Possition!

  1. Static : This is the by default possition of every image or object .
  2. Relative: this will not loss his possitional space , but it can move left,right,up or down by maintaining his own possitional space .
  3. Absolute: This will not able to hold his possition anymore ,it will start form the root of the page or from the root of the current parent div, but it can move itself from left to right and up to bottom.
  4. Sticky: This is a fixed possition on its div range . while scrolling the whole page up to bottom .
  5. Fixed: it will fixed all over the page while we scrolling up or down the page at we want for , but the image are fixed over the page here .
